The association's newspaper
 
 



  The Association has its own newspaper called  “La Feuille de Chêne”  (The Oak Leaf) which is an informative publication for members and sponsors alike.  It has been going strong since 1991. Around 3000 copies are printed quarterly.  All the articles are written by members of the association who can give their opinion and share their experience and knowledge with everyone.

The subjects discussed are very varied :

* The Association’s activities.
* Ideas.
* Important regional events concerning the environment.
* Mediterranean flora and fauna.
* Traditions and history of the Montagne Sainte-Victoire and surrounds.
* Dates of outings, tree planting, site maintenance.
