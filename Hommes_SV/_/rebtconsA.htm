Scientific and technical advisers 

 

Michel Thinon, ecologist at the C.N.R.S.  (National Centre for Scientific Research), member of the Committee in his own right, has initiated the other members of the Association in the methods to be used on these areas and has generally supervised all the reforestation.
 
 

Pierre Champroux, a technician on the Forestry Commission, also a Committee member in his own right, who  has successfully tested an acorn planting cane, helps in the supervision of our tree planting programme.
