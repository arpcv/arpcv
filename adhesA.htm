Join the ARPCV...
 
 



The AR.P.C.V. is actively engaged in Nature Conservancy , particularly that of the Montagne Sainte-Victoire and its surrounds   (reforestation of burnt sectors with deciduous species, site maintenance, the publication of books and CD-Roms about it).
Thanks to the two botanical walks it has created, the public can learn about Mediterranean flora and trees.  The Association also contributes to the environmental education of children from Aix and the surrounding region through free outings and video shows etc.  that it organises for schools
The A.R.P.C.V. is at present working on a large-scale project for the creation of an arboretum which would be a special reserve for Mediterranean species.

If you would like to support us in our action, don’t hesitate !!  Join us today and become, you too, a member of the A.R.P.C.V.
