The ARPCV recognised by French ministries...

 


The ARPCV received two official agreements in 2003 for its activities in environment and in education: one from the Ministry of the Environment and one from the Ministry of Education.

TheARPCV also received the label “thank you, says the planet” from the Ministry of the Environment .  The award was given to the association for its “Nature  Day”  (La Fête de la Nature) and the ceremony was held on 3rd July 2001 at the regional headquarters of the Ministry of the Environment under the presidency of the Prefect of the region.

Such a distinction only goes to confirm the quality of the A.R.P.C.V.’s action in favour of the environment
