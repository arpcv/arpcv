Publications by the A.R.P.C.V.

One of the goals of the ARPCV is to present to a wide public the importance and  beauty of Sainte-Victoire. For that purpose, the association has written and  edited two very complete documents on the mountain. (only available  in French).

Sorry, all these books are not available any more..

* Book :  « Sainte Victoire, discovery of a footpath »

  This book deals with the different topics raised by discoveries made on the discovery footpath created by the association. Subjects such as botany, geology and archaeology, dinosaurs and present day animals, history of people and traditions are developed. Beautiful pictures illustrate the texts, and a map helps the visitor on his walk.

* CD Rom on Sainte Victoire :

  The CD-Rom presentation of the mountain offers two aspects :
-  A sort of game in which for the different subjects encountered you must reconstitute all the operations needed to bring them to conclusion.  The subjects related to the mountain are for instance, bringing dinosaur’s eggs to Aix museum, making wine, making a picture like Cezanne…
- A very precious source of information about Sainte Victoire, in which 160 subjects are developed, and illustrated by around 1000 pictures.

This CD-rom was awarded a special prize in 1999 by the regional council, as the best publication related to environmental education.
 

* In the environmental domain, the association has also edited the first “regional guide to the courses dedicated to  the environment.”

This is the first directory which contains all the courses (232) dedicated to the  environment in Provence , the  Alps, and the  Côte d’Azur region. For each topic, the required standard of entry, the type and duration of courses, as well as the jobs that can be found at the end, are described.
 
 


Projects:

Creation of a Mediterranean arboretum near Sainte Victoire :

The main new project of the association is to create an arboretum dedicated to Mediterranean trees and vegetation at the foot of Sainte Victoire.

An arboretum is a park dedicated to  the  protection, development and conservation of tree species.

It would  then have:

- An educational role :  helping people to identify familiar or rare trees.
- A scientific role : studies of species, attempts to acclimatise foreign species.
- A role of conservation (certain species are threatened to disappear)

The association would like such a site to be open to the public at large , so that it could become a tourist attraction for people visiting Sainte Victoire as well as for local people. Many jobs could be created around this project, such as  a visitor’s  centre, tourist guides, maintenance of the site …
 

For this purpose, the  ARPCV plans to buy a 5 to 20 hectares ground close to the mountain. Since its creation, the association has been saving money in order to develop this project. Nevertheless, such a project cannot succeed without financial support from public organisations as well as from private sponsors.

If you are interested in participating in such a dynamic project, or only wish to discuss  it, please contact the association.
