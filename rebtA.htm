The goal of the association is not to reforest all the mountain or to take the place of the public environmental  institutions, but rather to experience new techniques of tree planting on dispersed areas ; and consequently to create zones that are less sensitive to the fire propagation, in parallel with the work of olive-trees planting done by the public institutions.
