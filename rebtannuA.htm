Annual tree planting 

plantations  november 2001 

Every year around November 25th , the A.R.P.C.V. organises a huge tree planting day where 1000 – 2000 trees are planted within the locality  on public land by 200 volunteers under the supervision of members of the Association.

* Such an impact could never be made without the financial support of our sponsors :

 Banque Populaire Provençale et Corse
 Conseil Général des Bouches du Rhône
 Gaz de France
 Patagonia  (American Foundation)
 STMicroelectronics   - Rousset

* Plus the hard work of many volunteers mainly belonging to sister associations such as Les Amis de la Nature, A.E.P. (a ramblers club) as well as the staff from the sponsoring firms. A lot of children participate in these Tree Planting Days when planting a tree takes on a highly symbolic meaning.

The planting itself is done by groups of 10-12 people supervised by a member of the A.R.P.C.V. responsible for one particular sector.  The supervisor makes sure there is a balanced mixture of differing species and that each tree is planted correctly (which means most of all that its protective covering is fixed properly so that there is no danger of it blowing away in strong mistral wind).

 More than 100 000 trees have been planted by the Association on the mountain ridge of Sainte-Victoire.
