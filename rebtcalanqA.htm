Reforestation in the Calanques 

 
 

The association’s methods are making news …

The success of these planting days and the association’s know-how has prompted other associations interested in the conservation of Nature to ask for aid.  Hence the ARPCV has carried out large reforesting operations in other parts of the region ravaged by fire.

In 2000, for instance, the association organised the reforestation of Sormiou in the Calanques near Marseilles, at the demand of local associations.
