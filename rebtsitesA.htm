Reforested sites

 

* The sites reforested by the Association are sites belonging to the public sector  i.e. ones to which the public have free access.

* The first reforestation programmes were carried out on the Cengle, a chalky plateau on the southern side of the Montagne Sainte-Victoire.

* A loan of land from the locality of Puyloubier on the south eastern side of the mountain has permitted for the creation of a botanical garden containing the different types of trees and plants found in the region.  Visitors, particularly groups, can go round the footpath which signposts the varying species.

* For several years now, reforestation has been effected on ground belonging to the Foreign Legion at Puyloubier which is state-owned.  We would like to express our gratitude to the management of this institution for its support and we appreciate the loan of tractors and tools as well as use of its grounds for a reception at the end of each annual replanting.
